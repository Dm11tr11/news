Greenhill Park Gets a Makeover

By Dmitrii

Greenhill Park, a popular hangout for families and students, just reopened after a six-month renovation project. The park now features a brand-new skatepark, updated playground equipment, and a fitness trail.

“We wanted to create a space where everyone feels welcome,” said local council member Jane Rivers. “These changes were designed based on community feedback.”

The park will also host events this summer, including movie nights and live music on Saturdays.
